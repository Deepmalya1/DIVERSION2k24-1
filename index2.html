The Mountains around the globe

Let's see the world with us with you and your family.

Swiss Alps

Discover the untamed beauty of Swiss Alps, a land of rugged mountains . The Swiss Alps also have 48 mountain peaks which are over 4,000m, as well as countless lakes and an extraordinary variety of flora and fauna.

Ladakh

Ladakh, often referred to as "the land of high passes," is a captivating tourist destination nestled in the northern part of IndiaRenowned for its breathtaking landscapes, unique culture, and ancient monasteries

Mount Klimanjaro

Mount Kilimanjaro, located in East Africa, is the highest free-standing mountain in the world and the tallest peak on the African continent.

Darjeeling

Darjeeling, a picturesque hill station located in the Indian state of West Bengal, is nestled in the foothills of the Eastern Himalayas. Famous for its lush tea plantations, breathtaking landscapes, and colonial charm.

Mount Rainier

Mount Rainier is the highest volcanic peak in the contiguous United States. It has the largest alpine glacial system outside of Alaska and the world's largest volcanic glacier cave system

Rocky Mountains

The Rocky Mountains are the easternmost portion of the expansive North American Cordillera. They are often defined as stretching from the Liard River in British Columbia south to the headwaters of the Pecos River, a tributary of the Rio Grande, in New Mexico.
